Iced Protein Coffee

Description

Indulge in a refreshing and protein-packed beverage with our Iced Protein Coffee recipe! Perfect for a post-workout boost or a morning pick-me-up, this creamy and energizing drink combines the rich flavors of coffee with the goodness of protein to keep you fueled and satisfied throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 scroop flavoured protein powder (any non-fruity flavour usually works well)
  • 500ml lowest fat chilled milk you can find
  • 2tbsp instant coffee powder (adjust to taste and caffeine requirement)

Steps

  1. Measure out protein powder and coffee poweder into shaker
  2. Measure out milk into shaker
  3. Add ice if you like
  4. Lid the shaker and shake till arm about to fall off.
  5. Devour
